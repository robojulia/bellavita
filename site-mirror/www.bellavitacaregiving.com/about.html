ABOUT US



Bella Vita Caregiving Services LLC. (BVCS) is a full-service non-medical home care provider with offices in Lycoming County, Pennsylvania. As a woman-owned business, BVCS was founded out of a need for providing superior non-medical in-home care services to patients in the Williamsport, Pennsylvania and surrounding areas. With round the clock coverage available, we offer adults (18+) with assisted services including but not restricted to: cooking, light housekeeping, laundry, medicine reminders, bathing, dressing, and attending doctor's visits.



Bella Vita Caregiving Services is a licensed home care agency providing high-quality care for those in need. We are compassionate and seek to ensure that each of our clients receives kind, tender and qualified tailored care for their unique needs and in their preferred surroundings.

CLIENTS WE SEE



ALZHEIMER'S PATIENTS





For those struggling from Alzheimer's and Dementia, you might be dealing with an increased disconnect from people and things you used to value most. Your normal daily routines become increasingly difficult to maintain. We're here to tell you that you are not alone in this. Bella Vita's dedicated staff provides a familiar face to help you or your loved one tackle each day.



COMPANION CARE





Depressed and anxious individuals often have a hard time completing basic daily tasks. Our team of skilled home health aides and certified nursing aids assist, guide and encourage our patients with every day chores, personal hygiene, meal preparation and leisure activities. Social interaction and companionship benefits the human psyche, which in turn creates greater empathy, higher self-esteem, more trust and cultivates healthy relationships.



PHYSICALLY DISABLED





Bella Vita also supports disabled adults who struggle with physical disabilities. Our team's focused approach can help you to love life again and learn the skills you need to cope with varying circumstances through with proper support, guidance, and trust.



SENIOR ASSISTANCE





Getting older can sometimes make normal day-to-day items increasingly taxing on your body and cognitive function. This is especially true when you feel increasingly alone due to life circumstances. At Bella Vita, we provide assistance and companionship to make the remaining years of your life as golden as possible.



COGNITIVE DISORDERS





Not all disabilities are visible. Bella Vita supports a wide range of patients with cognitive and behavioral mental health disorders that impair basic day-to-day function. Curious to see if you or a loved one qualifies? Give us a call today!



PARKINSON'S DISEASE





Parkinson's disease is one of the leading neurodegenerative diseases - coming in second to Alzheimer's. Here at Bella Vita, we are very experienced in this illness and assist our patients in managing their symptoms, with the hopes of slowing cognitive decline. No treatment can slow, reverse or stop this disease. But with compassionate care and a focus on healthy living, individuals with Parkinson's can still live satisfying and fulfilling lives.

WHERE WE'RE LOCATED



Williamsport, Lycoming County, Pennsylvania



Bella Vita Caregiving Services is located in Williamsport, PA.


​Williamsport is the main city in the roughly 114,000-person Williamsport-Lock Haven area, which spreads across both Lycoming and Clinton counties. Williamsport serves as the cultural, financial, and commercial center point of North Central Pennsylvania given its relative equidistance of 131 miles (211 km) from Philadelphia, 166 miles (267 km) from Pittsburgh, and 85 miles (137 km) from Harrisburg.


Known as the home of Little League Baseball, Williamsport contains a vibrant cultural scene with streets dotted in historic architecture, including Victorian-era homes and the impressive Williamsport City Hall. It's also situated along the West Branch of the Susquehanna River, offering scenic views and outdoor recreational opportunities.


As Central Pennsylvania's commercial, financial, and cultural hub, it's no wonder that Williamsport is home to a large volume of patients in need of home care services. To learn more, reach out to our team today.
